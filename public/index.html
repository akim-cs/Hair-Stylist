About Me

Welcome to my world of hair artistry. I'm an aspiring software developer hustling as a barber on the side with 5+ years of experience cutting hair.


I specialize in modern trendy cuts and styles so whether you're looking for a burst, shadow, taper-fade, a modern mullet, or even just a maintenance trim you'll be in great hands.


I believe that great hair should be an extension of your personality, so between our pre-cut consultation and the actual haircut itself you can expect me to be in your service for about an hour.


Feel free to come in with reference videos, pictures, TikToks, IG reels. I'm here for all of it. I want to help make your vision come to life.
